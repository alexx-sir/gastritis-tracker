Important: on PPIs and acid medication

Capellan cautions against long-term PPI use. For nighttime protection, he recommends short-term H2 blockers (famotidine). Sucralfate can be used short-term as a physical protective barrier that adheres to the stomach lining — not acid-based, so it works regardless of acid levels.
