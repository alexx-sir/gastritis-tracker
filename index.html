Food guide — Capellan protocol

Capellan's rule: choose foods with pH above 5, low fat, and easy to digest. Avoid anything that increases acid, irritates the mucosa, or slows gastric emptying. Your tolerance will be individual — track reactions in your daily log.

Supplement protocol

Capellan's science-backed supplement stack for protecting and repairing the gastric mucosal lining. Always check with your doctor, especially if on other medications.

Important: on PPIs and acid medication

Capellan specifically cautions against long-term PPI use. For nighttime protection, he recommends short-term H2 blockers (famotidine) over PPIs, as they preserve daytime digestive function. Sucralfate can be used short-term as a physical protective barrier that adheres directly to the stomach lining — not acid-based, so it works regardless of acid levels.
